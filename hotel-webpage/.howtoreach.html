HOW TO REACH US

BY ROAD

Nainital is connected by good roads to all the major national & state highways. It takes about7 to 8 hours to travel the distance between Delhi & Nainital (320 Kms.). Taxis are readily available from Delhi, and there are some overnight private luxury buses, as well as state run buses, which start from the Inter State Bus Terminus (ISBT) at around 9 P.M and reach Nainital early in the morning.


BY TRAIN

The nearest railhead is Kathgodam (34 Kms.). It takes one hour to drive up from Kathgodam to Nainital by taxi.

By Air
 The nearest airport is Pantnagar (70 Kms). Presently,AIR INDIA Airlines operates flights between Delhi & Pantnagar. The flight departs from Delhi at about 1 pm and reaches Pantnagar at about 2 pm. It takes about 2 hours to drive from Pantnagar to Nainital.
